I am Kaixin Yang (Chinese name: 杨凯欣), a Ph.D. student in DesCyPhy Lab at University of Southern California (USC), advised by Prof. Pierluigi Nuzzo. I am interested in hardware security and trustworthy hardware design. Recently, I focus on logic locking for gate-level VLSI design, Satisfiability (SAT) based attacks and Machine Learning (ML) based attacks on encrypted design, metrics for evaluating and guiding trustworthy design, and design space exploration for risk-free hardware design.
